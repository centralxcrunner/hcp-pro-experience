Step 6 — After the Call

Two Paths: Booked or Not Booked

Every call ends in one of two states. Both have an AI-driven follow-up strategy. No lead is left behind.

Booked a Job

  • SMS confirmation sent immediately — opens the ongoing comms channel
  • Ask for photos of the issue — gives pro context before arrival
  • Appointment reminders — day before + morning of
  • Ensure customer "sits" — proactive comms make the pro look professional. Reduces no-shows, improves sales conversion.
  • SMS = primary channel for all future comms
All automated by AI

Didn't Book

  • AI lead nurture pipeline — auto-works the lead
  • Days 1-3: Active SMS/call follow-up to re-engage
  • If no book: Transition to SMS/email drip campaign
  • Low-touch, regular cadence — stays warm for future need
  • Every contact enters the system permanently
All automated by AI

Feb 2026: Majority of new enrollees have no website
Of 2,866 pros enrolled in February 2026, only 48.4% have a website — the first month where the majority of new enrollees lack one. March is tracking even lower at 42.4%.

Website Adoption by Enrollment Year

Each data point shows the % of pros from that enrollment year who have a website today. The steady decline indicates a structural shift in who is joining HCP, not just a maturation lag.

This Isn't Just a Maturation Effect

Older cohorts do gain websites over time — roughly 3-4 percentage points per year of maturation. But the year-over-year decline is accelerating far beyond what maturation can explain.

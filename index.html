Value first, details second

CSR AI understands the issue and finds availability before asking for name/address. The customer knows they're getting helped before giving any personal info.

Pro context = confident answers

"We absolutely service Scottsdale" and "opening tomorrow morning" — specific, confident responses because CSR AI has the pro's service area and real-time calendar.

Natural conversation, not a form

Feels like talking to a friendly receptionist, not filling out fields. Empathy ("that's miserable in this heat"), confirmation, smooth close.

2 minutes → booked job

From "my AC isn't working" to a confirmed appointment in under 2 minutes. No hold music. No voicemail. No callback.
